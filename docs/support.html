General Questions

What is Affirm?

Affirm is a daily affirmations app that helps you build a positive mindset through beautiful, swipeable cards. It includes 200+ pre-loaded affirmations, the ability to create custom affirmations, streak tracking, widgets, and more.

How much does it cost?

Affirm is free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

What devices are supported?

Affirm requires iOS 17 or later and works on iPhone and iPad.

Do I need to create an account?

No! Affirm works immediately without any signup or login. Your data stays on your device.

Getting Started

How do I use the app?

  1. Open the app and complete the quick onboarding
  2. Swipe left or right to navigate through affirmation cards
  3. Double-tap a card to save it to your favorites
  4. Tap the shuffle button to randomize the deck
  5. Explore the tabs at the bottom: Home, Favorites, Categories, Create, Settings

How do I favorite an affirmation?

Double-tap any card or swipe up to add it to your favorites. You'll see a heart animation confirming it was saved.

How do I create a custom affirmation?

  1. Tap the Create tab (+ icon)
  2. Type your affirmation (up to 200 characters)
  3. Select a category
  4. Tap "Save Affirmation"

Your custom affirmation will appear in the deck and in your custom affirmations list.

Features

What are the 8 categories?

  • Self-Love: Embrace who you are
  • Confidence: Believe in yourself
  • Gratitude: Appreciate life's blessings
  • Success: Achieve your goals
  • Health: Nurture your body
  • Relationships: Connect with others
  • Morning: Start your day right
  • Evening: End your day peacefully

What is the streak feature?

The streak tracks how many consecutive days you've used the app. Open the app and view at least one affirmation each day to maintain your streak.

Milestones: Celebrate achievements at 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 90, 100, and 365 days!

How do I set up daily reminders?

  1. Go to Settings tab (gear icon)
  2. Enable "Daily Reminder"
  3. Choose your preferred time
  4. Grant notification permission when prompted

How do I add widgets?

  1. Long press on your home screen
  2. Tap the + button in the top left
  3. Search for "Affirm"
  4. Choose small, medium, or large widget
  5. Tap "Add Widget"

Troubleshooting

My streak reset - why?

Streaks reset if you don't open the app for a full day. Make sure to open the app and view at least one affirmation each day to maintain your streak.

I'm not receiving notifications

  1. Go to iPhone Settings > Affirm > Notifications
  2. Make sure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  3. Check that your notification time is set correctly in the app
  4. Make sure Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications

How do I backup my data?

Your data is automatically backed up if you have iCloud enabled on your device. You can also export your custom affirmations from Settings > Export Affirmations.

How do I delete my data?

To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. To delete specific items:

  • Favorites: Swipe left on any favorite and tap Delete
  • Custom Affirmations: Swipe left in the Create tab and tap Delete
  • Journal Entries: Swipe left on any entry and tap Delete

Privacy & Security

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, track, or share any of your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the app use iCloud?

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your favorites and custom affirmations will sync to your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and only accessible by you.
