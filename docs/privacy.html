Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Introduction

Affirm ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our daily affirmations app.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Affirm stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Custom Affirmations: Affirmations you create
  • Favorites: Affirmations you mark as favorites
  • Journal Entries: Your personal reflections
  • Mood Logs: Your mood tracking data
  • Usage Statistics: Streak count, views, and activity data
  • App Preferences: Settings like notification times, theme preferences

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT require an account or login
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your custom affirmations and favorites are synced to your personal iCloud account
  • This data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud storage
  • We do NOT have access to your iCloud data
  • Only you can access this data across your devices
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • We send local notifications on your device at times you choose
  • Notifications contain affirmation text
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

Widgets

If you add widgets to your home screen:

  • Widgets display affirmations from your local database
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Widgets update automatically using local data

Siri Shortcuts

If you use Siri Shortcuts:

  • Siri processes your voice commands locally on your device
  • We do NOT receive or store your voice data
  • Shortcuts access your local affirmations database
  • No data is sent to external servers

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage (SwiftData)
  • Your device's built-in security features protect your data
  • We do NOT transmit your data over the internet (except iCloud sync if enabled)
  • We do NOT store your data on external servers

Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • Deleting the app removes all local data
  • iCloud data (if enabled) remains in your iCloud account until you delete it
  • You can manually delete custom affirmations, favorites, and journal entries at any time

Children's Privacy

Affirm is suitable for users of all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

Third-Party Services

Affirm does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors (app is free)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data (via Settings > Export Affirmations)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app or individual items)
  • Disable iCloud sync
  • Disable notifications
  • Use the app without creating an account

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@affirmapp.com
Website: https://affirmapp.com

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Summary - In Plain English

Affirm is a privacy-first app. Everything stays on your device. We don't collect, track, or share any of your data. Your affirmations, favorites, and journal entries are yours alone. If you enable iCloud, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account - we never see it. No account required. No tracking. No ads. Just positive affirmations.
